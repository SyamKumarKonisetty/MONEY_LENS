# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: June 20, 2026

MoneyLens ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in the MoneyLens mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Summary of Our Privacy-First Commitment
- **No Cloud Servers**: MoneyLens has no external servers, database clouds, or backend networks.
- **Offline Storage**: All data, including transactions, budgets, settings, and profile details, is stored locally on your device.
- **No Tracking**: We do not collect analytics, tracking telemetry, search patterns, device identifiers, or usage statistics.
- **Zero Data Sharing**: Your personal and financial information is never sold, traded, or shared with third parties.

## 2. Information We Access and Process

### A. SMS Messages (Optional)
If you grant the **READ_SMS** permission, the App scans incoming text alerts from financial institutions to automatically detect, parse, and record transaction amounts, categories, and merchants.
- **Local Processing**: All parsing and extraction logic is performed entirely on your device.
- **No Transmission**: SMS contents are never transmitted over the internet or uploaded to any server.

### B. Device Storage (Optional)
If you export your data to CSV or import a CSV backup, the App accesses your device's local storage to save or read the backup file.
- **CSV Data**: The exported CSV file contains your transactions and budget ledger in plain text. Keep this file secure as it contains financial details.

## 3. Data Deletion and Retention
You have full control over your data:
- All data is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.
- You can instantly delete all recorded transactions, categories, budgets, and PIN hashes by tapping **Clear All App Data** in the Settings screen.

## 4. Updates to this Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App and will take effect immediately.
