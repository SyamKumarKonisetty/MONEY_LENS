# Google Play Store Assets Manifest

## Application Listing Metadata

*   **Application Name**: MoneyLens
*   **Short Description**: Secure, offline-first personal finance companion and SMS transaction tracker.
*   **Long Description**:
    MoneyLens is a premium, privacy-first, offline-first personal finance application designed to help you understand your spending habits, manage budgets, and track wealth without compromising your data.
    
    ### Key Features
    - **Offline-First Architecture**: Your financial data never leaves your device. MoneyLens operates completely offline, requiring no account creation, cloud syncing, or internet access.
    - **Smart SMS Ledger**: Automatically parses incoming bank alerts and transaction notifications locally on your device to categorize and register transactions in real-time. No manual entry required.
    - **Precision Budgeting**: Set monthly spending limits per category, track active spending via visual rings, and receive timely notifications as limits approach.
    - **Comprehensive Analytics**: Visualize your monthly cashflow, spending trends, and category breakdowns through premium, interactive charts.
    - **Secure Backup & Restore**: Export your localized ledger as a password-free, standard CSV file to save locally or share securely.
    - **PIN & Recovery Protection**: Protect your financial dashboard with a secure 4-digit PIN, complete with progressive lockout retry limits to prevent brute-force attacks.
