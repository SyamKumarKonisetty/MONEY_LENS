# 26: Marketing

## Purpose
To document the marketing strategy and store positioning for MoneyLens.

## Core Messaging Pillars
1. **Privacy Absolute**: "Your money, your device. Nothing leaves your phone."
2. **Zero Friction**: "It tracks so you don't have to."
3. **Visually Stunning**: "Finally, a finance app that feels like a premium wallet."

## Store Listing Assets
- **Feature Graphic**: Should showcase the glassmorphism Dashboard with the rolling balance counter.
- **Screenshots**: Must include text callouts pointing out the "Offline First" nature.

## Target Channels
- Privacy-focused subreddits (`r/privacy`, `r/degoogle`).
- Design communities (Dribbble, Twitter UI threads).
- Personal finance communities (`r/personalfinanceindia`).
