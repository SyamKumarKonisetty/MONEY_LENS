# 29: Future Ideas

## Purpose
A creative brain-dump for long-term enhancements outside the immediate roadmap.

## Ideas

### 1. Unified Receipt AI
Allow the user to forward PDF emails of online orders to a local device parser, or take a picture of a physical receipt, and use on-device ML to extract items and total amounts.

### 2. Location-Based Prompts
Geofence common spending areas (like a favorite coffee shop). When the user leaves the geofence, send a local push notification: "Did you just spend money at Starbucks? Log it now."

### 3. Gamified Savings
Introduce a virtual "pet" or "plant" that visually grows based on the user's adherence to their budget, adding a dopamine loop to financial discipline.

### 4. Shared Local Ledgers (P2P)
Use Bluetooth or Local Wi-Fi Direct to sync specific ledger segments between spouses, without ever routing the data through an external server.
